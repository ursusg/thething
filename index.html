About Me

Hey, what's up? I'm Ursus and I honestly don't know what to say about myself. I currently work as a freelance photographer. I've been taking photos for about 6 or 7 years now. I love photography and it started out as a hobby of mine when I was in highschool. It began with an ASM (After School Matters) program that taught students how to develop rolls of film and then develop prints inside of a darkroom. A print in the darkroom honestly made me more excited about photography than I've ever felt.

Other than photography, I tend to play a ton of video games and play the guitar. Right now, I mainly play Rocket League which is a car/soccer game. You drive around and have a rocket attached the back of the car. It allows for aerial movements and makes the game have the best first person perspective of being a player on a field. I've been playing for over a year or two at this point.

Oh, and I try to play my guitar as much as possible. I tried taking classes but found it way more engaging to learn on my own from Youtube videos or just from user-made tabs I found online. I understand having a foundation through classes makes for a better guitarist but I've always seen it as a hobby and less a career.
